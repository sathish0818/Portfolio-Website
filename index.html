Sathish Sachithanandam UX/UI Designer

Hello! I'm Sathish — a Chennai-based UX/UI designer with 8+ years of experience designing intuitive digital products for web and mobile. I specialise in crafting user-centric interfaces, building scalable design systems in Figma, and transforming complex workflows into clean, engaging experiences.

My process covers the full design spectrum — UX research, wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity UI design across SaaS platforms, healthcare applications, and brand projects. Staying ahead of industry trends, especially in AI-driven design, is part of how I work. Known for attention to detail and a strong collaborative approach, I deliver designs that look great and perform even better.
